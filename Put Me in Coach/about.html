About 'Put Me in Coach'

'Put Me in Coach' is a web application that our team developed for our Object Oriented Design computer science class at Louisiana State University. What began as a small team of 4 regular college kids grew into the same exact team that helped create this project, but with a lot more knowledge. Nonetheless, our team strived for the absolute best, so we created this web application capable of allowing our users to quickly create and find pickup sport matches in their area. We hope that our users can find people that have the same interests and passions as themselves so that they may spend less time trying to find and organize a match, and more time enjoying the free time they have!
